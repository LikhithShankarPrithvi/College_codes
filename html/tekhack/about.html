SKILL LOG: Build for people who requires a team to implement Aspiring ideas!!
ABOUT: Skill log is the technical interface which is built by AI.The main goal of skill log is to provide and integrate a team with suitable skills, for people having aspiring ideas.
Skill log collects the user information and gives a skill rating based on the prominent skills, idealogy and interpersonal stability of the person.This data is analysed and maintained based on user activity.
Skill log implements log search from the big data collected and provide a suitable partner or a member for the team which is grouped to implement the idea for a problem!!
Skill log recognizes the problem strength, analyses team requirements and suggests a team based on locality,communication versatility & people having similar perspective to the problem.
